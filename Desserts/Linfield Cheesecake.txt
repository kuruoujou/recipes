Linfield Cheesecake

Crust:
1 cup flaked coconut
1/4 cup chopped macadamia nuts
2 tbsp butter, melted

Filling:
16 oz cream cheese
1 cup sugar
3 tbsp GF mix
3 eggs
1 cup sour cream
3 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp pineapple or almond extract
8 oz crushed pineapple (drained)

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Grease a 9 in. springform pan

Crust:
Combine coconut and macadamia nuts in a small bowl
Stir in butter
Press onto the bottom of the pan
Bake for 10 min

Filling:
Beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth
Add flour mix and beat well
Add eggs, beating on low until just combined
Add sour cream, lemon juice and flavorings. Beat until just blended
Stir in drained pineapple with a mixing spoon

Pour filling over crust.
Bake for 50 min or until center is almost set
Cool and refridgerate for four hours to overnight


GF Mix:
2 parts rice flour
2/3 part potato starch
1/3 part tapioca flour

Source: Gluten Free Comfort Foods
