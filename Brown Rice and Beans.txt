Brown Rice and Beans


1 cup uncooked rice
1 can black beans
1 medium onion
3/4 cup salsa
1 tablespoon olive oil

Cook rice according to package directions. Coarsely chop onion. Add oil to medium skillet, and sauté onion until it just begins to turn clear. Add beans (drained), rice, and salsa. Stir and cook over medium heat until heated through, about ten minutes. 

Serve.
