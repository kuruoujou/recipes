Makes one 13 inch diameter crust.
    
1 tbsp yeast
2/3 cup warm water (110-115 F)
1/4 cup tapioca flour
1/4 cup sorghum flour
2 tbsp milk powder
2 tsp xanthan gum
1 egg
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
1 cup brown rice flour
1 tsp Italian seasoning

* Add 1/3 cup brown rice flour to a bowl with rest of ingredients
* Mix until smooth
* Stir in enough brown rice flour to make a dough
* Roll out dough and let rest for 10 min
* Bake 10-12 min or until golden brown
